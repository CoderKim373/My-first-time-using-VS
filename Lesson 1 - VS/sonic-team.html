The Sonic Team

Sonic the Hedgehog

Sonic is the main protagonist of the series, a blue hedgehog known for his incredible speed. He fights against Dr. Eggman to protect the world and his friends. Sonic's catchphrase is "Gotta go fast!"

Miles "Tails" Prower

Tails is a two-tailed fox and Sonic's best friend. He's a genius inventor and mechanic who can fly by spinning his tails. Tails often provides technical support and gadgets for the team.

Knuckles the Echidna

Knuckles is a red echidna and the guardian of the Master Emerald. He's strong and powerful, often acting as a rival to Sonic but ultimately a loyal ally. He's known for his strength and digging abilities.

Shadow the Hedgehog

Shadow is a dark hedgehog created by Professor Gerald Robotnik. He has chaos powers and is incredibly fast. Shadow is mysterious and often operates alone, but can be an ally when needed.
